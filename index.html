C.A Foundation

Overview: CA Foundation is an entry-level examination by ICAI (Institute of Chartered Accountants of India) assessing fundamental knowledge in accounting, business, economics, and commercial laws.

Skills Development: Develops skills in accounting principles, business laws, economics, and quantitative aptitude crucial for a career in accountancy and finance.

Career Opportunities: Opens pathways to careers in auditing, taxation, finance, consulting, and corporate governance, with global recognition and diverse job prospects.

Duration: The CA Foundation course typically takes about 8 months to prepare for the exam after completing 4 months of study period.

Curriculum: Covers four subjects - Principles and Practice of Accounting, Business Laws and Business Correspondence, Business Mathematics and Logical Reasoning & Statistics, and Business Economics & Business and Commercial Knowledge.
